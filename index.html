Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: What is included in a pooja kit?

Each kit contains all essential items required for the specific ritual or festival, tailored to your tradition and region. A detailed list is provided on the product page.

Q2: How do I book a priest through your platform?

Simply select the “Book a Priest” option during your kit order, choose your preferred language and tradition, and we’ll match you with a verified priest.

Q3: Can I order just the pooja kit without booking a priest?

Yes! You can order only the pooja kit or add priest booking as per your need.

Q4: How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 2-3 days before your planned pooja to ensure timely delivery and priest availability.

Q5: Do you deliver to my city?

We currently serve major cities and towns across India. Enter your pin code at checkout to confirm delivery availability.

Q6: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallet payments.

Q7: What if I need help or have a special request?

Our support team is always here to assist you. Contact us via email, phone, or through the app for any special requirements or questions.
